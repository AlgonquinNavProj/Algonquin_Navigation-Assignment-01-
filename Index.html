Welcome to GonqNav

This program is a real-life tool that helps students navigate easily all throughout campus. GonqNav uses Algonquin College official maps. We are hoping to be live by Spring 2026 . We are hoping to support students and staff adequately, safely and easily.

We are a non-for-profit team that serves the community with essentials, to make the public day-to-day lives easier.

A little bit more about Algonquin College, it is a public college in Ottawa that provides hands-on learning and career-focused programs in technology, health, and many other fields.
