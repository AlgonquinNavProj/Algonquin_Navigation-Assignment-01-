Welcome to Campus Navigator

This program is a real-life tool that helps students navigate easily all throughout campus. Campus Navigator uses Algonquin College official maps. We are hoping to be live by Spring 2026 . We are hoping to support students and staff adequately, safely and easily.

We are a non-for-profit team that serves the community with essentials, to make the public day-to-day lives easier.

Navigate smarter. Discover routes, explore places, and get where you need to go — faster and easier than ever before.
